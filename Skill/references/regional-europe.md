**Switzerland**: Major hub for European wealth management, strong privacy laws, established family office ecosystem

**Monaco**: Tax-efficient jurisdiction, attracts UHNW families, luxury lifestyle appeal

**Luxembourg**: Fund structuring expertise, EU access, growing family office presence

**Germany**: Large domestic wealth base, conservative approach, strong industrial family wealth

**Netherlands**: Established financial services sector, pragmatic regulatory environment
